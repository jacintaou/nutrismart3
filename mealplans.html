🥗 Chicken Power Bowl

A high fibre meal supporting healthy weight management.

Ingredients

  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 cup spinach
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil dressing

Instructions

1. Grill chicken breast until fully cooked.

2. Cook brown rice according to packet instructions.

3. Slice avocado and tomatoes.

4. Add spinach into bowl and top with rice and chicken.

5. Drizzle olive oil dressing over the meal before serving.

🥩 Steak Recovery Plate

High protein recovery meal ideal for fitness and muscle support.

Ingredients

  • 200g lean steak
  • 1 sweet potato
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic seasoning

Instructions

1. Season steak with garlic and herbs.

2. Roast sweet potato for 35 minutes.

3. Grill steak until desired doneness.

4. Steam broccoli until soft.

5. Serve together with olive oil drizzle.

🍝 Mediterranean Pasta

Balanced vegetarian meal packed with fibre and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • Wholegrain pasta
  • Spinach
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic cloves

Instructions

1. Cook wholegrain pasta until tender.

2. Sauté garlic and tomatoes in olive oil.

3. Add spinach and cook lightly.

4. Combine pasta with vegetables.

5. Sprinkle feta cheese over the top before serving.
