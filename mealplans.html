🥗 Chicken Power Bowl

High fibre meal supporting healthy weight management.

Ingredients

  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 avocado
  • Spinach
  • Cherry tomatoes

Instructions

1. Grill chicken breast.

2. Cook rice.

3. Add vegetables into bowl.

4. Top with chicken and avocado.

5. Serve fresh.

🥩 Steak Recovery Plate

High protein meal supporting muscle recovery.

Ingredients

  • 200g lean steak
  • Sweet potato
  • Broccoli

Instructions

1. Season steak.

2. Roast sweet potato.

3. Steam broccoli.

4. Grill steak and serve together.

🍝 Mediterranean Pasta

Balanced vegetarian meal packed with fibre.
