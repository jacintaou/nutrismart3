Healthy Eating Hub

Interactive Guide to Healthy Eating & Nutrition

Click each card to reveal key nutrition concepts, tools and guides. This section is designed to improve nutrition literacy through interactive learning, quizzes and practical food knowledge.

Macronutrients (CLICK ME)

Protein builds muscle and repairs tissue. Carbohydrates provide energy for the body and brain. Fats support hormones and brain health.

Micronutrients (CLICK ME)

Vitamins and minerals support immunity, energy production, bone health and metabolism. Examples include iron, calcium and vitamin C.

Hydration Guide (CLICK ME)

Water supports digestion, energy levels and temperature control. Most people need 6–8 cups per day depending on activity level.

Balanced Plate Method (CLICK ME)

Half vegetables, one quarter protein, one quarter carbohydrates. This is a simple method to build balanced meals.

Food Groups (CLICK ME)

Food is grouped into fruits, vegetables, grains, protein and dairy. Eating a variety ensures nutrient coverage.

Healthy Eating Tips (CLICK ME)

Eat whole foods, reduce processed sugar, increase fibre intake, and aim for consistent meal timing throughout the day.

Common Nutrition Myths (CLICK ME)

Carbs do NOT automatically cause weight gain. Fat is NOT always unhealthy. Balance and portion control matter most.
