Healthy Eating & Nutrition Learning Hub

Click on each card to reveal detailed nutrition information. This module teaches key concepts in a structured, interactive way.

Macronutrients

  • Protein → muscle repair + growth
  • Carbohydrates → main energy source
  • Fats → hormones + brain function

Micronutrients

  • Vitamins → immune support + metabolism
  • Minerals → bones, nerves, oxygen transport
  • Examples → iron, calcium, magnesium

Hydration

  • Supports energy + concentration
  • Helps digestion and nutrient absorption
  • Aim for 6–8 cups per day

Balanced Plate

  • ½ vegetables or salad
  • ¼ lean protein
  • ¼ carbohydrates

Food Groups

  • Fruits & vegetables → fibre + vitamins
  • Grains → energy (whole grains preferred)
  • Protein foods → growth + repair
  • Dairy → calcium + bone health

Healthy Eating Principles

  • Focus on whole foods
  • Limit processed sugar
  • Eat fibre-rich meals daily
  • Stay consistent with meals

Food Labels

  • Check serving size first
  • Watch sugar + sodium levels
  • Compare per 100g values
  • Ingredients listed by quantity

Nutrition Myths

  • Carbs do NOT automatically cause weight gain
  • Fat is NOT always unhealthy
  • Detox diets are unnecessary
  • Balance matters most

Energy Balance

  • Calories in vs calories out
  • Weight change = long-term balance
  • Metabolism + activity affect usage

Fibre Benefits

  • Improves digestion
  • Stabilises blood sugar
  • Supports gut health
  • Found in plants, grains, legumes
