About NutriSmart

Enterprise Nutrition Technology

🌍 Solving A Global Problem

Poor nutrition is a growing global issue linked to obesity, diabetes and cardiovascular disease. Many individuals struggle to understand nutrition information and make healthy dietary choices.

💻 Enterprise Computing

NutriSmart demonstrates enterprise computing through data analytics, interactive dashboards, intelligent recommendation systems and responsive user-centred design.

📊 Data Visualisation

Users can visualise calories, hydration and wellness progress through graphs, progress trackers and interactive health metrics.

🔒 Secure Data Storage

NutriSmart uses browser local storage to securely store user preferences and health tracking data.

🤖 Intelligent Systems

The nutrition analyser uses data-driven logic to provide health ratings and suggest healthier food swaps.

🚀 Future Improvements

Future developments could include AI-powered meal recommendations, cloud databases, user accounts and wearable fitness integration.

Our Mission

NutriSmart aims to empower individuals to make healthier choices through accessible enterprise technologies, interactive nutrition insights and engaging user experiences.
